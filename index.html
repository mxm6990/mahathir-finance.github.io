About

A little about how I think and what I'm building toward.

I'm interested in the intersection of finance, technology, and data-driven decision making.

Right now I'm building financial analytics tools and exploring how systems can be optimized through data—from IT risk frameworks to investment analysis and user-centered financial applications.

I like taking ideas from concept to execution through hands-on projects with Python, data visualization, and web development. There's something satisfying about shipping something you can click through or rerun with new inputs.

Long term, I'm aiming toward fintech, financial analytics, and tools that scale—ones that solve real problems for real users, not just slide decks.
